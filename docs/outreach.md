# AutoFiveStar — Founding Client Outreach Kit

Lead-gen launch playbook. Every message points prospects at the free audit:
**https://www.autofivestar.com/free-review-audit**

The audit is the hook — it's free, takes two minutes, needs no account, and
gives you a reason to follow up with a real number about their business.

---

## Who to target first
Local service businesses that live on Google reviews and have visible gaps:
restaurants, contractors, salons, med spas, clinics, auto shops, dentists,
home services. Best early targets:
- 50–250 reviews (enough to matter, not so many they feel "done")
- Rating 3.8–4.5 (clear room to improve)
- Recent 1–2★ review sitting **without an owner reply** (your opener writes itself)

## After they run the audit (you get a lead-notification email)

The audit emails you the lead with business name, email, phone, city, and
score. Reply within the hour while it's warm:

> Hi {{FirstName}}, saw you ran the audit — your score came in at {{Score}}/100.
> The biggest quick win is usually {{topRecommendation}}. Want to hop on a
> 15-minute call and I'll show you exactly how we'd close the gap? We're taking
> a small group of founding clients at locked-in pricing right now.

Setup-call booking link: https://www.autofivestar.com/contact?topic=demo

---

## Talk track for the setup call (done-with-you positioning)
1. Walk through their audit score live.
2. Show the sample dashboard at https://www.autofivestar.com (demo data is
   labeled — frame it as "this is what yours looks like once connected").
3. Pitch the plan: Growth ($199/mo) is the default — includes done-with-you
   setup. Reputation Guard ($399/mo) for clients who want hands-on response
   help + a monthly strategy call.
4. Mention **founding client pricing** to create urgency.
5. Close: book the setup, collect payment, you connect their Google profile
   with them on the call.

---

## Note on "send your score" offers
Until Google approves the `business.manage` scope (GBP_LIVE), the in-app audit
runs on representative sample data, clearly labeled "sample preview." If you
offer to personally send a score, pull their real public Google rating/▢count
manually so the number you quote is real. The product fulfillment (live review
sync, AI drafts) comes online once GBP_LIVE + AI_LIVE are enabled.
